About
--------------------------------
Introducing our Wireframe Shader for Unity URP - a powerful and versatile shader solution that enables you to add stunning wireframe visual effects to your Unity projects. 
With its intuitive interface and customizable options, this shader makes it easy to add wireframe effects to your objects, and it's fully compatible with Unity URP, so you can use it in your high-performance projects with confidence. 
Whether you're creating an artistic wireframe representation of your models or adding an edgy touch to your game, this shader is the perfect choice for any project that demands stunning visual effects.


Installation Instructions
--------------------------------
1. Import the asset into your project.
2. Drag and drop the Wireframe material onto any mesh.
3. Configure the Wireframe shader settings from the editor.


Troubleshooting
--------------------------------
Q: My wireframe shader isn't casting shadows. What's wrong?
A: The wireframe shader will only cast shadows when set to Lit mode with Depth Write enabled and Cast Shadows enabled.

Q: How do I enable both sides of the mesh so that the wireframe shader draws the entire mesh?
A: Set the material's Culling property to Off.
